Founder

Wafaa Abd Alkareem Kwaiz

I am Wafaa, a polymer and petrochemical materials engineer and founder of the VADA project. During my studies, I knew the damages of paraffin that is used in most candles, which causes serious diseases over time. This was the reason why I started VADA project to make candles using environmentally friendly vegetable wax that is safe for human health.
